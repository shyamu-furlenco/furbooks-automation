----Plan Transition----
The transition from the current plan to the new plan will be results in change in price

The price change will be calculated as follows:

Start_Date          End_Date     tenure    taxable_amount_monthly(Rs.)      Plan_transitition_related_discount(Rs)      Other discounts(Rs)      Total_discount (Rs)

2024-07-04          2024-07-03      1           100                             0                                                  10                  10               
2024-07-04          2024-10-03      3           80                              20                                                 10                  30
2024-10-04          2025-10-03      12          70                              25                                                 15                  40


* Now, from first to second cycle the amount is reduced from Rs. 100 to Rs. 80, which means there is a price change of Rs. 20. This price change is due to the plan transition
* From second to third cycle the amount is reduced from Rs. 80 to Rs. 70, which means there is a price change of Rs. 10. This price change is due to the plan transition and other discounts. The plan transition related discount is Rs. 25 and other discounts are Rs. 15, which means the total discount is Rs. 40.
